Where does this distribution come from ?

You are using the simple english version of Wikimedia by moulin, targeting the non-native english-speaking world. This version is available as free download on the Internet from the moulin website (moulinwiki.org).

Community-specific versions exists and are distributed by other organisations like USAID in West-Africa.

This version has been issued in November 2007, with the Wikimedia projects' datas of November 16th 2007.
