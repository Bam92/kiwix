The other projects

From the different Wiki projects runned by the Wikimedia Foundation, Wikipedia is by far the most important.
But it's not alone !

Due to the huge success Wikipedia has encountered, the foundation has launched several sister projects like a dictionary, or a compendium of quotations.

Follow the links bellow to learn more about those side-projects

  • Wikiquote, a compendium of quotations from notable people.
  • Wikibooks, a collection of open-content textbooks.
  • Wiktionary, a dictionary that uses simple words so it is easier to understand.
