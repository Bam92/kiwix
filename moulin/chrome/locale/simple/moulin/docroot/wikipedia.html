How to get into Wikipedia ?

Wikipedia is like a giant spider of articles. It can be quite difficult to reach the information you want, just because you can't express it the right way. The fastest way to find informations is the search engine (at the top). Also, the various links on this page can help you start with subjects and categories which would drive you to more specific informations.
You may also want to take a look at the Very good articles category.
